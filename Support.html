Help, questions, and feedback

The best way to reach us is by email at heliplan@pm.me. We read every message and aim to reply within a few business days.

Common questions

The app can't find my location or weather.
HeliPlan looks up weather and elevation from public aviation and weather services over the internet. Make sure you have a connection. If a place name doesn't resolve, try an airport identifier or coordinates instead.
How do I let the app use my current location?
Tap the location button in the app. If you previously declined, enable location access for HeliPlan in your device's Settings under Privacy & Location Services. You can always enter locations manually instead.
Is my data stored anywhere?
Everything you enter stays on your device. There are no accounts and nothing is uploaded to us. The one exception, if you choose to use it, is that your aircraft, bucket, and equipment libraries sync across your own devices through your private iCloud account — handled by Apple, never seen by us. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Will my aircraft and gear show up on my other devices?
Yes, as long as those devices are signed in to the same iCloud account and have iCloud enabled for HeliPlan. Your aircraft, bucket, and equipment libraries sync automatically; mission inputs and settings stay local to each device. To turn syncing off, disable iCloud for HeliPlan in your device's Settings, or sign out of iCloud.
Where do the per-diem rates come from?
Lodging and meal rates come from the U.S. GSA and require an API key you add in Settings. Without a key, the rest of the app works normally.
I found a bug or have a feature request.
Please email us with as much detail as you can — what you were doing, what you expected, and what happened. Reports genuinely help.

A reminder about use

HeliPlan is a planning and reference aid, not a substitute for approved aircraft documentation, official weather sources, or your own training and judgment. Always cross-check every figure before flight. The pilot in command is solely responsible for the safe conduct of every flight.
